The Exchange is a central marketplace for carbon credits.

The Exchange has a traditional trading architecture and uses distributed ledger technology to securitize carbon credits into digital tokens (receipts) representing a clear line of ownership between the carbon credits held in the Trust and the digital receipt (Token).

Like in traditional commodity exchanges, owners of Tokens can request physical delivery of the underlying carbon credits.

SPOT EXCHANGE

Carbon is traded via standardized contracts with full control over bid/ask prices.

  • Fast trades

  • Instant trad settlement/clearing
Carbon Market Board (CMB)

A supercharged version of the OTC trade process. Sellers easily transact with trusted counterparties on a per project basis.

  • Buyers can purchase project specific credits with flexibility on volume and price.
  • Reduce paperwork and time spent negotiating OTC trades.
  • Quick and safe settlement with trusted counterparty.
Auctions

Auctions offer project developers an avenue for bulk sale of credits and price discovery for their assets

  • Sellers have full flexibility to set the auction date, auction duration, reserve price, bid volume, etc.
  • Reduce paperwork and time spent negotiating OTC trades.
  • Quick and safe settlement with trusted counterparty.
