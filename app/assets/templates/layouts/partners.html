"AirCarbon Exchange (ACX) enables TradeFlow to execute on a core part of our Climate change and ESG initiative which is to neutralise Shipping-related Carbon Emissions for the Commodity export trades we enable for SME firms around the world. Through ACX we buy Carbon Credits in the safe knowledge that these projects had been vetted before allowing them to be listed on the market. ACX also allows us unique access to emerging market based renewable energy credits that allow us to give give additional support to meaningful projects in the communities we partner with.”
Dr. Tom James, CEO & CIO, TradeFlow Capital Management
“We are thrilled to have worked on this project with ACX at a time when digital assets and sustainable business practices are rapidly being integrated into the global financial ecosystem. As a purely digital firm succeeding at a time when other sectors of the economy are facing challenges, we must find ways to give back to the communities in which we operate. I consider carbon offset a key initiative in our journey to sustainability and our evolution as a socially responsible business.”
Hugh Madden, CEO, BC Group
